   - Emails sent to {frontmatter.contactEmail}. Stored for up to{' '}
     {frontmatter.supportEmailRetentionMonths} months or until resolved.
3. E2EE content
   - Messages, files, and settings are E2EE (end‑to‑end encrypted) on the
     {frontmatter.iotaName} and your device. We cannot access plaintext.
   - Routing metadata (e.g. sender/recipient identifiers) is exchanged when using the {frontmatter.omikronName} but it does not log or store it.
4. Technical and analytics
   - Analytics: {frontmatter.analyticsEnabled ? 'Enabled' : 'None'}.

#### E2EE and keys

- Keys are currently {frontmatter.keyGeneration}. You are responsible for
  backing up your private key.
- If a third‑party {frontmatter.iotaName} generates or retains your private
  key, they could decrypt your content. Prefer self‑hosting or generating your private key and then migrating to another Iota.
- The private key hash ({frontmatter.privateKeyHashAlgo}) verifies
  authenticity. We never store your plaintext private key.

#### {frontmatter.omikronName} relay

- Connects you to other users and your own {frontmatter.iotaName}
- Hides your IP from other users and acts as a reverse proxy so you don’t
  need to open ports on your firewall. Communication is not logged or stored.
- You may choose to not use a {frontmatter.omikronName} (future option), which
  may expose your IP to peers.
